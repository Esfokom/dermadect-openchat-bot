Dermadect Bot - Your AI-Powered Healthcare Companion

Welcome to Dermadect, where cutting-edge AI meets healthcare expertise. Our bot represents the future of digital healthcare assistance, combining advanced artificial intelligence with medical knowledge to provide personalized health support.

Available Commands:

1. `/prompt` - Start a symptom-based health consultation
   - Describe your symptoms
   - Receive intelligent follow-up questions
   - Get condition assessment
   - Ask follow-up questions
   - Example: "/prompt I've been having headaches and fatigue"

2. `/game` - Interactive anatomy learning game
   - `/game help` - Get game instructions
   - `/game start game` - Begin a new game session
   - `/game end game` - End current game and view stats
   - Track your progress and scores
   - Learn through interactive questions

3. `/health_tip` - Get daily health tips
   - Wellness advice
   - Preventive care information
   - Lifestyle recommendations
   - Evidence-based health content

4. `/health_joke` - Get medical-themed humor
   - Light-hearted healthcare content
   - Stress-relieving jokes
   - Educational entertainment

5. `/info` - View this information
   - Learn about available commands
   - Understand bot capabilities
   - Get usage instructions
